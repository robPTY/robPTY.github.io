Optimizing Poultry Processing Efficiency: Automation Leveraging CNN

January 30, 2025
Annual STEM Posters at the Capitol, Little Rock, AR

Presented my research on automating Tyson Foods' poultry sample processing method using a YOLO v10 model, a CNN optimized for object detection. The model was trained and deployed on OpenCV, achieving around 90% mAP, though improvements are still in progress.
